Visualisation

Many tools exist to calculate physicochemical properties of protein structures.
pdb-vis brings several of those tools together and provides convenient visualisations of protein structure properties at the residue level.

For each chain the following properties are calculated and mapped onto the sequence:

  • DSSP secondary structure converted to 6 states (β-strand, α-helix, 310-helix, π-helix, turn, and loop)
  • residue molecular accessible surface area (Å2)
  • whether the residue is clearly solvent accessible. A residue is defined as clearly exposed if it is more exposed than 10 Å2, or more exposed than a third of its accessibility in the unfolded state
  • whether the residue contacts symmetry-related residues

The number of symmetry contacts is visualized for each residue in a YASARA scene. Download the freely available YASARA view to inspect the scene.
